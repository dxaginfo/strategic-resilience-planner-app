About Strategic Resilience Planning
Why Strategic Resilience Matters

In sports organizations, over-reliance on star players, key coaches, or pivotal executives can create significant vulnerability. When these individuals are unavailable (due to injury, retirement, or departure), organizations without resilience planning face:

  • Performance decline
  • Financial instability
  • Loss of strategic direction
  • Damage to brand identity
  • Diminished fan engagement
How Our Assessment Works

This tool helps you identify dependency risks by evaluating the impact of key personnel across five critical domains:

  1. Performance Impact: Direct contribution to competitive results
  2. Revenue Generation: Impact on financial performance and revenue streams
  3. Brand Value: Contribution to organizational identity and public perception
  4. Knowledge Concentration: Possession of unique or undocumented knowledge
  5. Decision Authority: Level of control over critical decisions

After assessment, you'll receive a comprehensive analysis and customized recommendations to build strategic resilience.
